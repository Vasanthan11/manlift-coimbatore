Boom lift range

Boom lift types available for different sites

Pick the right boom lift configuration based on how high you need to reach, how far you need to outreach and whether work is indoor, outdoor or a mix of both.

Articulated booms

Knuckle booms for up-and-over access

Articulated booms bend at one or more points, allowing the basket to reach around corners, over roofs and under beams. Ideal when there are structural obstacles between the machine and the work area.

Approx. 40–85 ft · Indoor & outdoor models · Good for tight sites

Telescopic booms

Straight booms for maximum outreach

Telescopic booms extend in a straight line to reach long horizontal distances. They are preferred for large, open sites such as industrial plants, yards, tank farms and bridges.

Approx. 60–135 ft · Strong horizontal reach · Open work fronts

Hybrid booms

Low-noise booms for sensitive areas

Hybrid boom lifts reduce emissions and noise, making them suitable for malls, airports, manufacturing floors and other semi-indoor spaces with people and equipment nearby.

Non-marking tyres · Lower noise · Emission-sensitive jobs

Height & reach planning

Information we use to size boom lifts

A few simple details are enough to avoid under-sized or over-sized boom lifts on site. You don’t need detailed drawings – even rough dimensions help.

  • Maximum height from finished floor level to the highest work point.
  • Approximate horizontal distance from machine parking area to the work point.
  • Floor type and allowable load along the access path and at the working zone.
  • Obstacles in the way: canopies, parapet walls, pipelines, conveyors, etc.
  • Whether you need our operators or plan to use your own trained crew.
