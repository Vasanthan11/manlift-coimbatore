Examples of calls we handle every day

When you contact us, you don’t need a perfect technical description. A simple explanation of your work is enough to begin.

  • “We’re changing façade panels on a completed building for the next two weeks.”
  • “We have a warehouse with 11m racks and need to use lighting above the aisles.”
  • “Our site has partly finished floors and open areas – we’re not sure what mix we need.”
  • “We’ve already used one type of lift and want to check if there’s a safer or faster option.”

If you’re unsure how to describe your requirement, start with height, photos (if possible) and a rough sketch of the work area – we’ll take it from there.
