Rental playbook

A simple flow to plan your access equipment

You can use this 4-step outline with your internal teams. Most of our blog posts connect back to one or more of these steps.

Step 01 · Define your work points

Height, area and number of locations

List the highest work points, note how many areas are involved and whether they are indoors, outdoors or mixed. Even rough sketches or photos are useful.

Step 02 · Match equipment type

Boom, scissor or man lift?

Use blog posts tagged “planning” to see when you should choose a straight-up platform, an up-and-over boom lift or a man lift for moving locations.

Step 03 · Decide duration & shifts

How long do you really need the lift?

Group tasks and plan shift timings so that access equipment is used steadily. The “cost & optimisation” posts suggest typical durations for common project types.

Step 04 · Prepare for safe use

Safety, checks and responsibilities

Use our safety articles and daily checklists to integrate the lift into your permit-to-work, toolbox talks and site induction process.
